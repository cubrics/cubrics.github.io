What we do and how we do it


We achieve the goal


Our purpose is to realise our client’s business value by collaboration and partnership. We identify, prioritise, design, deliver and increase the adoption of digital transformations at pace and thus lowering your transformation costs. As experienced consultants, project and change managers and therapists in cognitive behavioural therapy, we were acutely aware of the gulf that frequently exists between an organisation’s transformational goals and the knowledge, skills and expertise necessary to achieve them.

We do it for less


Bringing in outside assistance or the lack of stakeholder management to drive through necessary change was often part of the problem. Inexperienced or under-qualified consultants increased the administrative workload, pushing up costs and even drawing out delivery times. More generalised consultancies simply lacked the specialised talent to bring to bear on clients’ projects.

We are more efficient


With a pragmatic and highly efficient approach to problem solving, enabling change adoption and an established track-record of delivering first-class projects for partners that have included Shell, Sky, AstraZeneca and the Guardian newspaper group, we were convinced that we could offer clients better value.



And we still are.

We bring the experience


Drawing on methodologies developed, tested and refined across five continents and in over fifty countries, we provide a streamlined suite of services that can be applied at all or any stages of the transformational journey.

We unlock your potential


Our shared mission over the years hasn’t changed: Helping our clients to harness the power of cutting-edge technologies and ingrain change into their organisation, unlocking their potential and readying them for the challenges ahead in an evolving market environment.
