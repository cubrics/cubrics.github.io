About Us
WHAT WE DO – AND WHY WE DO IT

As experienced consultants and project managers, we were acutely aware of the gulf that frequently exists between an organisation’s transformational goals and the knowledge, skills and expertise necessary to achieve them. Bringing in outside assistance to drive through necessary change was often part of the problem. Inexperienced or under-qualified consultants increased the administrative workload, pushing up costs and even drawing out delivery times. More generalised consultancies simply lacked the specialised talent to bring to bear on clients’ projects. With a pragmatic and highly efficient approach to change management, an established track-record of delivering first-class projects for partners that have included Shell, Sky, GSK and The Guardian newspaper group, we were convinced that we could offer clients a better deal. And we still are. Drawing on methodologies developed, tested and refined across five continents and in over fifty countries, we provide a streamlined suite of services that can be applied at all or any stages of the transformational journey. Our shared mission over the years hasn’t changed: Helping our clients to harness the power of cutting-edge technologies and ingrain change into their organisation, unlocking their potential and readying them for the challenges ahead in an uncertain world.
