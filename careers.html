Careers

Graduate programme

If you are an ambitious graduate who is interested in starting a career in digital transformation, we would love to hear from you. Successful applicants will follow a development plan designed to nurture your unique strengths and give you the support you need to grow with the company. You will take on a variety of roles, helping to ensure that client projects are smoothly executed. The Cubrics consultant is a smart, digitally-literate and highly organised self-starter who relishes in solving problems.

assignment
Assessment

Each graduate will be assessed on their chosen field (Business Analysis, Programme Management or Change Management) and the highest performers will be placed based on skillset suitability.

model_training
Training

As we work together, our company’s vision is to ensure you grow with us and accomplish modern practices to deliver the right solution for our clients. We encourage and support our employees to be certified in professional training courses such as APMG Change Management, PRINCE2 Project Management and AGILE methodologies.

Experience

You will be exposed to various roles within our programmes, internally and at clients’ projects. These range from strategic planning to technical deployments, across one or more functional business area.

Progression

Each field has multiple opportunities for progression and our aim is to ensure you flourish in your role. Your experience will be gained from organisations in various industries in multiple disciplines of their business.
